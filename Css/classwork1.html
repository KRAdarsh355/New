GOOGLE PIXEL 9A


Rs 39,000

The Google Pixel 9a is a mid-range smartphone featuring a 6.3-inch 120Hz OLED display, the Tensor G4 chipset, and a massive 5,100 mAh battery.

It boasts a 48MP main camera, IP68 water resistance, and an aluminum frame. Starting at $499, it includes 7 years of software support.

The Google Pixel 9a delivers decent casual gaming performance, but it is not designed to compete with dedicated gaming smartphones. While its Google Tensor G4 chipset manages everyday tasks flawlessly, it struggles to match the raw graphical power of flagship alternatives like Apple's A18 or Qualcomm's Snapdragon
