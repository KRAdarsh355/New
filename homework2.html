Title: Harry Potter
Author: J.K. Rowling
It's a fantasy novel that follows the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends as they attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The series explores themes of friendship, bravery, and the battle between good and evil.


Title: The Great Gatsby
Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald
This classic novel is set in the Roaring Twenties and tells the story of the mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his obsession with the beautiful Daisy Buchanan. The novel explores themes of wealth, love, and the American Dream.


Title: To Kill a Mockingbird
Author: Harper Lee
This classic novel is set in the American South during the 1930s and follows the story of Scout Finch as she grows up and learns about racial injustice and moral integrity.
